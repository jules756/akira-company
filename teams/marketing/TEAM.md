Head of Marketing runs the weekly content calendar (3 posts/week — Mon lead-magnet / Wed personal / Fri technical) and the Monday review-and-adapt ritual. Content Writer produces posts, newsletter issues, cold emails, proposals. Reports to CEO via Head of Marketing. Primary project: `demand-creation`.
