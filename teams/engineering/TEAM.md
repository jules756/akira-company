The Engineering & Product team takes a signed Proposal and turns it into a Production voice + email + booking bot for a Nordic restaurant or hotel. CTO locks plans and gates reviews. Founding Engineer builds library skills (build once, deploy N). Implementation Engineer deploys any library skill to any client. UX Designer maintains the design system and reviews voice-agent UX. Product Manager owns the "Agents (The Bots)" Notion DB end-to-end and audits each new client Proposal against the skill library — reuse first, build only where the library doesn't cover.

Reports to CEO via CTO. Primary project: `skill-library-delivery`.
