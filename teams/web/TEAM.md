The Web team owns akira-agent.com end-to-end with a Next.js + Vercel single stack.

- **SEO + AEO Specialist** briefs every article and tracks both Google rankings and AI search citations.
- **SEO Trend Researcher** scouts the SEO + AEO landscape daily and feeds tactic updates to the team — major Google or LLM platform updates get a response plan within 48h.
- **Web Writer** ships 2 articles per day, 7 days a week (14/week target) from SEO + AEO briefs, plus case studies for every Production client.
- **Web Designer** creates page templates and landing pages using v0.dev + Vercel-native components.
- **Web Operations** owns all Vercel deployments (including magnet pages on the same Vercel project), plus GA4 + GTM + Microsoft Clarity instrumentation, plus **daily Search Console analysis**.

Reports to Head of Marketing. Primary project: `web-publishing`.
