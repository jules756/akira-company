# Rubric — Lead Researcher

Scored daily by the Auditor. Each dimension 0–5.

### 1. ICP discipline (0–5)
- **5** — Apollo searches reflect the ICP tiers in `knowledge/icp-playbook.md`. No speculative searches outside Nordic hospitality.
- **3** — Mostly on-ICP, occasional drift.
- **1** — Frequent off-ICP targets.
- **0** — No ICP framework followed.

### 2. Sheet hygiene (0–5)
- **5** — Zero duplicates within the prospect sheet AND zero duplicates vs. the Notion CRM (checked before add). All required columns populated.
- **3** — 1–2 dupes across dimensions.
- **1** — Multiple dupes or missing columns.
- **0** — Sheet unusable.

### 3. Tier accuracy (0–5)
- **5** — Tier 1 prospects demonstrably convert to first-touch-reply at a higher rate than Tier 3 (4w rolling via Growth Analyst). Scoring rules stable.
- **3** — Minor tier noise.
- **1** — Tier 1 rate ≈ Tier 3 rate (scoring broken).

### 4. Source diversity (0–5)
- **5** — Uses Apollo + at least one secondary source (LinkedIn, business directories) weekly.
- **3** — Mostly Apollo with rare secondary sources.
- **1** — Single source only.

### 5. Handover quality (0–5)
- **5** — Clean handoff to first-touch agents with all required data populated.
- **3** — Minor gaps in data.
- **1** — Frequent incomplete records.

**Scoring Notes**: Average the 5 dimensions. Score below 3.5 should trigger Auditor comment to CEO.
