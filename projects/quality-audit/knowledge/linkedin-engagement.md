# Rubric — LinkedIn Engagement Agent

Scored daily by the Auditor. Each dimension 0–5.

### 1. Volume adherence (0–5)
- **5** — 5 engagement actions/day landed (comments + reactions on target posts), no misses in last 7 days.
- **3** — 3–4 actions/day average.
- **1** — <3 actions/day.
- **0** — Silent.

### 2. Language discipline (0–5)
- **5** — Every comment matches the language of the post being commented on (Swedish / English / French only). Every DM matches prospect's profile language. **Zero Norwegian outputs.**
- **3** — 1–2 language mismatches in the week.
- **1** — Multiple mismatches OR a Norwegian output.
- **0** — Systematic language failure.

### 3. Brand safety (0–5)
- **5** — Every post and DM passed brand-voice-enforcement draft review; Jules approved before publishing. Auto-comments stayed within allowed topics (restaurant/hotel ops, AI in hospitality, Nordic SMB tech).
- **3** — One off-topic auto-comment or one approved-but-should-not-have-been post.
- **1** — Brand safety breached once.
- **0** — Repeated breaches.

### 4. Engagement quality (0–5)
- **5** — Comments generate replies OR measurable profile-view lift. Rolling 4w reply rate per comment trends up.
- **3** — Consistent baseline reply rate, no drift.
- **1** — Declining reply rate.
- **0** — Zero replies.

### 5. Target relevance (0–5)
- **5** — Every action is on a post by a Nordic hospitality operator or a decision-maker we've identified as ICP. No "just to stay active" noise.
- **3** — Most actions on-ICP, some drift.
- **1** — Frequently off-ICP.
- **0** — Random engagement.
