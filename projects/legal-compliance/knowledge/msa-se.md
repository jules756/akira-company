## 1. Scope of Services

1.1 The Provider shall deliver the AI agent services described in **Exhibit A (Scope)**: **{{scope_summary}}**.

1.2 Services are delivered as a managed SaaS offering hosted on infrastructure controlled by the Provider. Delivery includes agent configuration, a client-specific knowledge index (produced by the Provider's Email Crawler from Client-authorised mailboxes), a dedicated client environment, and ongoing maintenance.

1.3 Any change in scope requires a written amendment signed by both Parties.

## 2. Term and Termination

2.1 **Initial Term.** This Agreement is effective from **{{effective_date}}** for an initial term of twelve (12) months.

2.2 **Renewal.** At the end of the Initial Term, this Agreement renews automatically for successive twelve (12) month periods unless either Party gives written notice of non-renewal at least three (3) months before the end of the then-current term.

2.3 **Termination for cause.** Either Party may terminate this Agreement with thirty (30) days written notice if the other Party commits a material breach that is not cured within such notice period.

## 3. Fees

3.1 **Installation fee.** One-time fee of **{{installation_fee}} SEK** payable within 14 days of the Effective Date.

3.2 **Monthly subscription.** **{{monthly_fee}} SEK** per month, invoiced monthly in advance.

3.3 **VAT.** All fees are exclusive of VAT (Moms 25%), which will be added to each invoice.

3.4 **Payment terms.** Net 14 days from invoice date. Late payments accrue interest at the rate set by the Swedish Interest Act (`Räntelagen`).

## 4. Data Protection

4.1 The processing of personal data under this Agreement is governed by the separate **Data Processing Agreement (DPA)** entered into between the Parties, which forms an integral part of this Agreement.

4.2 The Client is the data controller; the Provider acts as data processor for the personal data processed on the Client's behalf.

## 5. Intellectual Property

5.1 **Provider IP.** The Provider retains all right, title, and interest in its generic agent skills, software, models, templates, and supporting infrastructure (the "Provider IP"). The Client is granted a non-exclusive, non-transferable licence to use the Provider IP for the duration of this Agreement.

5.2 **Client IP.** The Client retains all right, title, and interest in its data, including but not limited to its knowledge index, configuration values, communications content, and customer data (the "Client IP").

5.3 Nothing in this Agreement transfers ownership of Provider IP to the Client or vice versa.

## 6. Confidentiality

6.1 Each Party agrees to keep the other Party's Confidential Information in strict confidence and to use it only for the purpose of performing its obligations under this Agreement.

6.2 The confidentiality obligation survives termination of this Agreement for a period of five (5) years.

## 7. Limitation of Liability

7.1 Neither Party shall be liable to the other for indirect, incidental, consequential, or punitive damages.

7.2 Except for breaches of confidentiality or data-protection obligations, each Party's aggregate liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid by the Client to the Provider in the twelve (12) months immediately preceding the event giving rise to the claim.

## 8. Governing Law and Venue

8.1 This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Sweden, without regard to its conflict-of-laws provisions.

8.2 Any dispute arising from this Agreement shall be submitted to the exclusive jurisdiction of Stockholm District Court (`Stockholms tingsrätt`).

## 9. Language

9.1 This Agreement is executed in English. Any translation is provided for convenience only; the English version governs in case of conflict.

## 10. Entire Agreement

10.1 This Agreement, together with its Exhibits and the DPA, constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties regarding its subject matter and supersedes all prior agreements.
