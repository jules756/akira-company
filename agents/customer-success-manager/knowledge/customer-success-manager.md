# Rubric — Customer Success Manager

Scored daily by Auditor. Each dimension 0–5.

### 1. Retention scan discipline (0–5)
- **5** — Every Production / Maintenance client scanned every heartbeat. `client-health.md` updated daily. No stale rows.
- **3** — Most clients scanned but occasional misses.
- **1** — Irregular scans.
- **0** — Scans stopped.

### 2. Churn-signal responsiveness (0–5)
- **5** — Every churn signal flagged same heartbeat with specific signal + recommended action + urgency. Zero signals sit unread for >24h.
- **3** — Most flagged but occasional delays.
- **1** — Signals missed or diluted.
- **0** — Signals silently ignored.

### 3. Library-driven upsell execution (0–5)
- **5** — Every new capability triggers a same-heartbeat sweep. Every eligible client gets a subtask to Head of Sales with rationale tied to a specific client signal. No capability rollout has sat without a sweep for >24h.
- **3** — Sweeps happen but sometimes delayed or lack specificity.
- **1** — Sweeps irregular.
- **0** — Skills ship without sweeps.

### 4. 90-day referral discipline (0–5)
- **5** — Every Production client at the 90-day mark gets a referral ask triggered within 7 days of the anniversary. Zero missed.
- **3** — Most hit, some miss by a week.
- **1** — Multiple missed.
- **0** — Referrals never asked.

### 5. Retention brief quality (0–5)
- **5** — Friday brief shipped on time. All sections filled with reconciled numbers (Production / Maintenance / MRR / NRR / upsells / churn flags / referrals). Every number cites source.
- **3** — Shipped but incomplete.
- **1** — Late or missing sections.
- **0** — Not shipped.
