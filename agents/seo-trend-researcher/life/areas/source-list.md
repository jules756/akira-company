## What this list does NOT include

- Generic AI news (we're not chasing AI hype, we're chasing SEO/AEO tactics).

## How to add a source

Two qualifications:
1. The source consistently publishes actionable SEO/AEO tactics (not just news).
2. We've actioned ≥1 idea from them in the past 3 months.

Drop sources that fail either after 3 months.
