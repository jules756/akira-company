## 1. Purpose

The Parties wish to exchange confidential information in connection with evaluating a potential commercial relationship regarding AI agent services for hospitality operations (the "Purpose").

## 2. Confidential Information

2.1 "Confidential Information" means any information disclosed by one Party (the "Discloser") to the other (the "Recipient") that is marked as confidential or that a reasonable person would understand to be confidential given the nature of the information and the circumstances of disclosure. This includes, without limitation, business plans, pricing, technology, customer lists, operational data, and know-how.

2.2 Confidential Information does not include information that: (a) is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the Recipient; (b) was rightfully known to the Recipient before disclosure; (c) is independently developed by the Recipient without reference to the Discloser's Confidential Information; or (d) is rightfully obtained from a third party without a duty of confidentiality.

## 3. Obligations

3.1 The Recipient shall:

- Use the Confidential Information only for the Purpose.
- Protect the Confidential Information with at least the same degree of care it uses for its own confidential information of similar nature, and in no event less than reasonable care.
- Limit access to the Confidential Information to its employees, contractors, and advisors who have a need to know for the Purpose and are bound by confidentiality obligations at least as protective as those in this NDA.
- Not disclose the Confidential Information to any third party without the Discloser's prior written consent.

## 4. Term

4.1 This NDA is effective from the date first written above and continues for two (2) years, unless terminated earlier by either Party on thirty (30) days written notice.

4.2 The confidentiality obligations in Section 3 survive termination for a period of three (3) years from the date of disclosure of the relevant Confidential Information.

## 5. Return of Materials

5.1 Upon request or upon termination of this NDA, the Recipient shall promptly return or destroy all Confidential Information in its possession, except to the extent retention is required by law or by the Recipient's routine backup procedures.

## 6. No Licence

6.1 Nothing in this NDA grants either Party any right or licence in the other Party's intellectual property beyond the limited right to use Confidential Information for the Purpose.

## 7. No Obligation to Contract

7.1 This NDA does not obligate either Party to enter into any further agreement or business relationship.
