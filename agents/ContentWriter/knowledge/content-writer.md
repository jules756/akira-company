# Rubric — Content Writer

Scored daily by the Auditor. Each dimension 0–5.

### 1. Brand voice consistency (0–5)
- **5** — Every piece passes `brand-voice-enforcement` checks. No "we're thrilled to announce" corporate filler.
- **3** — Occasional voice drift.
- **1** — Frequent filler or mismatched tone.
- **0** — Voice undefined or ignored.

### 2. Hook strength (0–5)
- **5** — First line of every post stops the scroll. Growth Analyst's rolling 4w post-stop-rate trends up.
- **3** — Baseline hook quality, flat trend.
- **1** — Weak openings.
- **0** — Generic openings across the board.

### 3. Turnaround speed (0–5)
- **5** — Drafts delivered within one heartbeat of request; zero aged subtasks.
- **3** — ≤1 request aged >1 day.
- **1** — Multiple aged subtasks.
- **0** — Requests stall.

### 4. Template library growth (0–5)
- **5** — Every pattern that works twice gets written to `life/areas/templates/` with tags. Library compounds.
- **3** — Some templates saved, not systematic.
- **1** — One-off writing, no library.
- **0** — No templates.

### 5. Delivery surface quality (0–5)
- **5** — Emails clear subject + specific ask + short body. LinkedIn posts have one idea per post. Newsletter issues have one hook + one CTA.
- **3** — Mostly clean, occasional drift.
- **1** — Kitchen-sink pieces.
- **0** — No clear delivery discipline.
