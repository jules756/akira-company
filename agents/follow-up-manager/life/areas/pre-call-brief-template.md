# Pre-call Brief Template

> Owned by Follow-Up Manager. Used every time a demo / Discovery call is booked via Cal.com. The brief is generated at booking time, attached to the Company's Notion page, and read by Head of Sales (or Jules) 2 minutes before the call.
>
> **Framework source:** Cold to Cash — *"L'intelligence pré-appel — Plus jamais un call à froid."* Adapted for Akira's stack.
>
> **Last updated:** 2026-04-16

## Trigger

Whenever Follow-Up Manager confirms a Cal.com booking (after an inbound lead reply or proactive outreach landed a meeting), immediately:

1. Generate the brief using the prompt below.
2. Submit a `[steward-write] append-page-body` request to CRM Steward to attach the brief to the Company's Notion page.
3. Post a comment on Head of Sales's "CRM Daily" issue: `Pre-call brief ready for <Company Name> — call <date/time>.`

## Inputs

Before running the prompt, gather:

- **LinkedIn conversation.** All LinkedIn DMs between Jules and the prospect (via Composio → LinkedIn).
- **Email conversation.** All Gmail threads matching the Contact Email (via Composio → Gmail).
- **CRM record.** The prospect's Company row in Notion — Contact Name, Restaurant Type, Locations, Pipeline Status, current Proposal relation if any.
- **Outreach Log history.** Every past touch (Channel, Date, Outcome, Notes) related to this Company.
- **Engagement history.** Any row in LinkedIn Engagement Agent's `engagement-log.md` naming this prospect (demand-creation layer — which of Jules's posts did they engage with?).
- **Prospect sheet row.** If this lead was sourced via the Google Sheet, its enrichment columns (Decision-maker Title, Source, Tier).

## Prompt

Submit to the LLM (opencode inherits the grok-4-1-fast model, or route via Composio if a different model is preferred):

```
You receive all available conversation context between Jules and {{prospect_name}}
at {{company_name}}, plus enriched prospect data from the CRM and prospect sheet.

Inputs provided:
- LinkedIn conversation: {{linkedin_conversation}}
- Email conversation: {{email_conversation}}
- CRM record: {{crm_record}}
- Outreach Log history: {{outreach_log}}
- Engagement history: {{engagement_log}}
- Prospect sheet enrichment: {{sheet_row}}

Generate a structured pre-call brief:

1. **5-point summary** — the key facts of the conversation so far. Facts only, no interpretation.
2. **Objections or hesitations raised** — what has the prospect questioned or pushed back on? Quote directly if possible.
3. **Interest level** — one of: Elevated / Moderate / Exploratory. Justify in one sentence.
4. **Company context** — size, segment, current stack (if known), any recent news or signals from LinkedIn/web.
5. **Probable call motivation** — why did they accept a call? Tie to a specific engagement or message if you can.

Format: bullet points, not prose. Maximum 200 words.
Language: match the prospect's primary language (Swedish / English / French). Never Norwegian.
```

## Output structure

The brief lands on the Company's Notion page under a new section, formatted in Markdown:

```markdown
## Pre-call Brief — <date> — <call type>

### 5-point summary
- ...
- ...
- ...
- ...
- ...

### Objections or hesitations
- ...

### Interest level
**Elevated | Moderate | Exploratory** — <one-line justification>

### Company context
- Size: <N locations, headcount if known>
- Segment: <Restaurant Type or Hotel subcategory>
- Stack: <known tech / booking platform / reservation system>
- Recent: <LinkedIn post / news / hiring signal if relevant>

### Probable call motivation
<one or two sentences tying to a specific prior engagement or message>

---
Generated by Follow-Up Manager on booking. Read before the call.
```

## Language rules

- Swedish-speaking prospect → brief in Swedish. Jules reads Swedish (treat as English-equivalent comprehension).
- French-speaking prospect → brief in French. Jules is French-native.
- English-speaking → English.
- Never Norwegian.

## After the call

Head of Sales appends a `Post-call notes` section to the same Company page via another `append-page-body` request — immediately after the call ends. This creates a searchable trail: every call has a pre-brief and a post-notes section on the Company's page, dated.

## Regeneration rules

If the call is rescheduled >48h later, regenerate the brief before the new date (the context may have shifted — new LinkedIn posts, new email exchanges, new signals). Otherwise one brief per call.
