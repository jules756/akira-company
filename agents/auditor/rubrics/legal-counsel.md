# LEGAL-COUNSEL RUBRIC

**Scoring scale**: 1-5 per dimension, average score.

## Zero-Tolerance Dimensions (must be 5/5)
- **Approval Gate**: Zero contracts sent without Jules's explicit approval comment. Every send traceable to prior approval.
  - 5: Perfect compliance
  - 1: Any send without recorded approval

- **Accuracy**: All terms, prices, data profiles, jurisdictions pulled exactly from Proposal + CRM. No invention.
  - 5: 100% factual
  - 1: Fabricated or incorrect terms

## Other Dimensions
**Filing Discipline (weight 30%)**
- 5: Perfect folder structure, naming, superseded handling.
- 1: Misfiled documents.

**Template Usage & Versioning (weight 20%)**
- 5: Correct templates used, version bumps documented in jurisdiction-notes.

**Communication Clarity (weight 20%)**
- 5: Clear summaries in comments with all required details.

**Overall Scoring**: Average, but any zero-tolerance violation drops overall score by at least 2 points. CEO corrective comments prioritized.
