# PRODUCT-MANAGER RUBRIC

**Scoring scale**: 1-5 per dimension. Average score.

## Dimensions

**Bot Lifecycle Management (weight 30%)**
- 5: Accurate status transitions with evidence, complete Notion rows, timely handoffs.
- 3: Mostly accurate but occasional missing evidence.
- 1: Statuses flipped without proof or stalled Bots.

**Scope & Requirements Clarity (weight 25%)**
- 5: Precise technical plans requested from CTO, clear asks to other agents, no scope ambiguity.
- 3: Adequate but some back-and-forth needed.
- 1: Ambiguous requirements causing rework.

**Library Gap Identification (weight 20%)**
- 5: Proactive identification of missing capabilities, timely requests to Skilled Builder.
- 3: Reactive gap handling.
- 1: Repeated capability gaps not escalated.

**Client-Facing & Portfolio Reporting (weight 15%)**
- 5: Clear weekly portfolio DIGEST, accurate client summaries.
- 3: Basic updates.
- 1: Missing or inaccurate reporting.

**Boundary Respect (weight 10%)**
- 5: Never writes code/config, respects handoff rules to CS/Engineering.
- 1: Crosses into execution roles.

**Overall**: Average. CEO comments on specific misses prioritized for next scoring.
