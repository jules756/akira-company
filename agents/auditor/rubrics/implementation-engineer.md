# IMPLEMENTATION-ENGINEER RUBRIC

**Scoring scale**: 1-5 per dimension.

## Dimensions

**Client Onboarding & Knowledge Validation (weight 30%)**
- 5: Thorough Stage-2 validation of knowledge index, accurate feedback collection, clear handoff.
- 3: Adequate validation.
- 1: Incomplete knowledge or missed client issues.

**Support Quality & Responsiveness (weight 25%)**
- 5: Rapid, accurate first-line support. Clear reproduction steps for bugs escalated to Founding/QA.
- 3: Reasonable response time.
- 1: Slow or incorrect support causing churn risk.

**Pattern Surfacing (weight 20%)**
- 5: Identifies recurring issues/patterns and escalates effectively to Product/CTO/Founding.
- 3: Some patterns noted.
- 1: No systemic learning from client feedback.

**Documentation & Client Communication (weight 15%)**
- 5: Excellent client notes, updates in client-health.md, clear status reports.
- 3: Basic.
- 1: Poor or missing client documentation.

**Boundary Respect (weight 10%)**
- 5: Never writes platform code, defers to Founding Engineer for core work.

**Overall Scoring**: Average. CEO corrective comments have priority.
