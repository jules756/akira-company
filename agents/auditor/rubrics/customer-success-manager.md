# CUSTOMER-SUCCESS-MANAGER RUBRIC

**Scoring scale**: 1-5. Average across dimensions.

## Dimensions

**Client Health Tracking (weight 35%)**
- 5: Living client-health.md perfectly maintained, accurate MRR/NRR, incident tracking, timely touches.
- 3: Mostly up to date.
- 1: Outdated or missing key metrics.

**Churn Prevention & Teardown (weight 25%)**
- 5: Proactive risk identification, clean handoff to Deployment Engineer on churn with all data export coordination.
- 3: Reactive.
- 1: Missed churn signals or incomplete teardown coordination.

**Upsell & Referral Identification (weight 20%)**
- 5: Accurate identification of expansion opportunities based on usage and feedback.
- 3: Some opportunities spotted.
- 1: No proactive upsell signals.

**Onboarding Handoff & Support (weight 20%)**
- 5: Smooth handoff from Implementation Engineer, clear communication, rapid response to issues.

**Overall Scoring**: Average. Track trend. Prioritize CEO corrective comments.
