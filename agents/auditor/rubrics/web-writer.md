# WEB-WRITER RUBRIC

**Scoring scale**: 1-5 per dimension. Average.

## Dimensions

**Content Quality & Clarity (weight 40%)**
- 5: Clear, engaging, error-free, on-brand, persuasive copy that drives action.
- 3: Serviceable but could be sharper.
- 1: Grammatical errors, off-brand, unclear.

**SEO/AEO Alignment (weight 25%)**
- 5: Incorporates target keywords naturally, follows SEO best practices from specialists.
- 3: Basic keyword use.
- 1: Ignores SEO guidance.

**Consistency & Voice (weight 20%)**
- 5: Maintains consistent brand voice across all pieces.
- 3: Minor inconsistencies.
- 1: Inconsistent tone/voice.

**Timeliness & Volume (weight 15%)**
- 5: Meets deadlines, high output without quality drop.
- 3: Occasional delays.
- 1: Misses deadlines frequently.

**Overall**: Average score. CEO feedback prioritized.
