# Rubric — Growth Analyst

> Scored weekly by the Auditor (Growth Analyst is weekly-cadence). Each dimension 0–5.

### 1. Friday Board Brief quality (0–5)

- **5** — Delivered by Friday. Contains: top-of-funnel metrics (prospects added, first-touches), pipeline state (count per stage, stuck leads), conversion rates (stage-to-stage, last 4 weeks), forecast vs. 500K SEK trajectory, 3 recommendations.
- **3** — Delivered but one section thin.
- **1** — Delivered late or missing two+ sections.
- **0** — Not delivered.

### 2. Measurement integrity (0–5)

- **5** — Numbers reconcile across Notion CRM, Outreach Log, and Google Sheet. Time ranges explicit. Definitions stable week over week.
- **3** — Reconciles but definitions drift slightly.
- **1** — Numbers don't reconcile.
- **0** — Ad-hoc numbers without traceable source.

### 3. Stuck-lead detection (0–5)

- **5** — Every lead stuck in a stage beyond threshold (Discovery >14d, Proposal >10d) is flagged by name with days-in-stage + last-touch date.
- **3** — Flagged in aggregate but not per-lead.
- **1** — Mentions stuck leads but misses most.
- **0** — No stuck-lead analysis.

### 4. Recommendations discipline (0–5)

- **5** — 3 recommendations per brief, each actionable, each tied to a metric, each with a proposed owner.
- **3** — Recommendations present but vague.
- **1** — Observations without recommendations.
- **0** — No recommendations.

### 5. Funnel coverage (0–5)

- **5** — Brief covers top-of-funnel (prospect sourcing → first-touch conversion) AND pipeline (first-touch → stages → close). Two flywheels measured.
- **3** — One flywheel well, other shallow.
- **1** — Only pipeline, nothing on top-of-funnel.
- **0** — Neither covered.
