# Rubric — Lead Magnet Creator

Scored weekly by the Auditor. Each dimension 0–5.

### 1. Ideation quality (0–5)
- **5** — `life/areas/magnet-pipeline.md` contains 3–5 ranked ideas weekly, each tied to a prospect question / trend / ICP pain. Ideas lean micro-product (tool / calculator / assessment), not just PDFs.
- **3** — Pipeline exists but ideas are mostly document-type or untied to evidence.
- **1** — Pipeline is thin or missing.
- **0** — No ideation work.

### 2. Ship rate (0–5)
- **5** — From "approved idea" to "live Tally magnet" ≤7 days for simple tools. Magnets hit publish without death-by-polish.
- **3** — 8–14 days.
- **1** — >14 days; or WIP graveyard.
- **0** — Nothing shipped this month.

### 3. Conversion rate per magnet (0–5)
- **5** — Rolling 4w conversion (email signup / view) averages ≥25%. Underperformers killed.
- **3** — 10–25% average.
- **1** — <10%.
- **0** — Not tracked.

### 4. Newsletter growth contribution (0–5)
- **5** — Magnets generate the majority of net new Beehiiv subscribers. Target: ≥50/week in the first 90 days (adjustable).
- **3** — Steady but below target.
- **1** — Minimal contribution.
- **0** — No subscriber attribution to magnets.

### 5. Library hygiene (0–5)
- **5** — `life/areas/magnet-library.md` lists every shipped magnet with URL, live-date, 4w conversion, total signups, status (live/killed). Underperformers marked `killed` with reason.
- **3** — Library exists but stale.
- **1** — Library partial.
- **0** — No library.
