# Rubric — Head of Marketing

Scored daily by the Auditor. Each dimension 0–5.

### 1. Content calendar discipline (0–5)
- **5** — Calendar published Mondays in `life/areas/content-playbook.md` with themes + owners + cadence, and actually executed.
- **3** — Calendar exists but misses ≥1 commitment per week.
- **1** — Ad-hoc; no forward plan.
- **0** — No calendar.

### 2. Learning loop (0–5)
- **5** — Weekly review on Mondays reads Growth Analyst's Friday brief, updates `content-playbook.md` with specific learnings (what worked / what didn't / why), and issues new directives to Content Writer + LinkedIn Engagement + Lead Magnet Creator.
- **3** — Review happens but rarely produces directives.
- **1** — Review is vague.
- **0** — No weekly review.

### 3. Delegation quality (0–5)
- **5** — Every subtask to Marketing reports has `parentId`, `goalId`, concrete ask, owner, deadline.
- **3** — Some subtasks vague.
- **1** — Reports guess what to work on.
- **0** — No delegation.

### 4. Metrics-driven decisions (0–5)
- **5** — Decisions cite specific metrics from Growth Analyst. No "gut" calls on content direction.
- **3** — Sometimes cites metrics.
- **1** — Decisions feel arbitrary.
- **0** — Ignores metrics.

### 5. Top-of-funnel velocity (0–5)
- **5** — Net new newsletter subscribers + prospects sourced by Lead Researcher trending up week over week.
- **3** — Flat.
- **1** — Declining without a stated plan to fix.
- **0** — Declining + no reaction.
