# Rubric — Lead Researcher

Scored daily by the Auditor. Each dimension 0–5.

### 1. ICP discipline (0–5)
- **5** — Apollo searches reflect the ICP tiers in `life/areas/icp-playbook.md`. No speculative searches outside Nordic hospitality.
- **3** — Mostly on-ICP, occasional drift.
- **1** — Frequent off-ICP targets.
- **0** — No ICP framework followed.

### 2. Sheet hygiene (0–5)
- **5** — Zero duplicates within the prospect sheet AND zero duplicates vs. the Notion CRM (checked before add). All required columns populated.
- **3** — 1–2 dupes across dimensions.
- **1** — Multiple dupes or missing columns.
- **0** — Sheet unusable.

### 3. Tier accuracy (0–5)
- **5** — Tier 1 prospects demonstrably convert to first-touch-reply at a higher rate than Tier 3 (4w rolling via Growth Analyst). Scoring rules stable.
- **3** — Minor tier noise.
- **1** — Tier 1 rate ≈ Tier 3 rate (scoring broken).
- **0** — No measurement.

### 4. Volume (0–5)
- **5** — Weekly prospects added hits the target set by Head of Sales (default 50/week until overridden).
- **3** — 75–100% of target.
- **1** — <75%.
- **0** — Nothing added.

### 5. Source diversity (0–5)
- **5** — Apollo is the spine, but at least one secondary source feeds the sheet weekly (LinkedIn search, local directories, partner lists). No single-source fragility.
- **3** — Mostly Apollo, occasional secondary.
- **1** — Apollo only.
- **0** — Inconsistent sourcing.
